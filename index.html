Why Journey with us?

Over 3 million pilgrims visit the Holy Land every year without incident. We take every precaution to ensure our pilgrims are Safe and secure.

Enjoy every Comfort of home, including high quality hotels, first-rate dining, state of art buses, and experienced, multilingual tour guides.

Live and experience the hallowed stones of Jesus' ministry and meet the "Living Stones", who have kept the faith for over 2000 years.

Your spiritual journey is our vocation. Our pilgrimages are 100% Mission-driven, allowing you to build your itinerary at an affordable cost.
